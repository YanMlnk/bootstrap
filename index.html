Top Albums of the Month

Seulgi "28 reasons"

The album as a whole is a striking balance between dark pop arrangements and smooth R&B with touches of dance beats. The darkness of tracks “28 Reasons” and “Los Angeles” are intoxicating with their moments of scarcity offset by epic builds nicely spaced throughout the tracks.

NME - 4/5 RS - 5/5 AOTY - 4/5
